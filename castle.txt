Castle is an American crime drama and police procedural television series that airs on ABC.

What happens when you pair a famous detective mystery writer with a New York City female detective? Mystery Novelist Richard Castle finds out when he is called in to help Detective Kate Beckett with a unique case of a murderer who seems to be committing copy-cat murders based on incidents in one of his best sellers.

The first episode of Castle aired March 9, 2009 and the last episode of Castle aired on May 16, 2016.
